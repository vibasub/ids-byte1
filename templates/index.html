2010 Bicycle Commuter Data in the United States

This is the Fusion Byte of the Data Pipeline class. The data set provides information on the number of bicycle commuters for 366 cities in the United States. Specifically, the columns in the dataset include city, state, population, total workers, percentage of bicycle commuters, number of bike commuters, percentage of bike commuters who are male, and percentage of bike commuters who are female.
